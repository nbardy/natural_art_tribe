Pachamama Collectivo

Pachamama, the goddess of the Incas, was the goddess of the earth. She was also the earth itself.

Pachamama Collectivo is a nomadic collective that brings together artists who thrive in nature. 1 month, 6-10 artists, 1 beautiful co-living house in a unique location chosen for it's abundance of natural beauty

Photographers, Poets, Authors, Painters who capture stories, landscapes, animals, and anything else that catches their eye

For those of us who go to the end of the earth for our art. We don't need to go alone anymore, let's find our tribe
