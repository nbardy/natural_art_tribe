Join Our Cohort in Paraty, Brazil!

Our first cohort will convene in the wonderful town of Paraty, Brazil in June 2025.

Paraty, Brazil, is a beautiful location in a remote town near Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Some of the opportunities available to artists include: world-class clear water for underwater diving, beautiful beaches and breaking waves for ocean/surf photographers, lush green landscapes and coastal mountain ranges for plein air painters and landscape photographers. Plus, the bustling city of Rio de Janeiro, with its overflowing culture and beauty, is only a 3-hour drive away. We can enjoy the combination of having a quiet place and being close enough to Brazil's cultural hub to visit and embrace all the aspects of this beautiful region.
